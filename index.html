Team It!

Team It! is an iOS app that helps organize meetings by finding the best times for everyone. The app matches schedules, saves meeting dates, and lets all team members view and share them!

Bite Bliss is an iOS app for food lovers to discover new restaurants. Swipe right to like or left to pass on suggestions.
